Multichannel extension for score-informed source separation using instrument spectral patterns


This page contains separation results for article "Multichannel extension for score-informed source separation using instrument spectral patterns" submitted to 19th International Conference Computational and Mathematical Methods in Science and Engineering.

Authors: A.J. Munoz-Montoro, D. Suarez-Dou, P. Vera-Candeas and J. Ranilla

Datasets
  • Sample 1: L. van Beethoven - Symphony no. 7, I movement, bars 1-53.
  • Sample 2: A. Bruckner - Symphony no. 8, II movement, bars 1-61.
  • Sample 3: G. Mahler - Symphony no. 1, IV movement, bars 1-85.
  • Sample 4: W. A. Mozart - The soprano aria of Donna Elvira from the opera Don Giovanni.
